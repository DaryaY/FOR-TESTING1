ADOBE AFTER EFFECTS

2D Animation and Motion Graphics

Top 5 Indie Games of 2017

A 30-second Motion Graphic made completely in Adobe After Effects, including the assets. Soundtrack used under Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported.

Created for a final project in Motion Graphics where students were tasked to create an animation that depicts “Top 5” of anything, with the following requirements:

  • Duration: 30 seconds
  • Format: HD, H264, 24 or 29.97fps
  • Opening and Ending: 2-5 seconds
  • Transitions between each content
  • Identifiers for each content

Kinetic Type: Poem “I hate”

A kinetic type of the poem "I hate" ("Я не люблю") by Vladimir Vysotsky (translated by Andrey Kneller). Created in Adobe After Effects, with visual assets designed in Adobe Photoshop and narrated by myself. Soundtrack used under Creative Commons Attribution Share-Alike (3.0) license.

Made for a Motion Graphics class where students were tasked with designing a kinetic type animation of the poem of their choice.

Obstetrics and Gynecology: Mitosis, Meiosis, & Aneuploidy

An Animation that follows a recorded narration and script. Original assets were created in Adobe Illustrator and a 2D animation was made of them in Adobe After Effects.

The animation was created for Stamford Hospital as part of the “2017 APGO Medical Education Endowment Fund Grant" application. The video serves as a general review for medical students on the topic of Genetics of Obstetrics and Gynecology.

Motion Portrait: Nina Drozdenko

Created by combining ”Green Screen” footage of Nina Drozdenko with original assets designed in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe After Effects.

A project for Motion Graphics class where students had to design a strictly formatted, promotional video of another student in the class.
