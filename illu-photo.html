Futuristic Poster: Implants

Utilized Adobe Illustrator to create the hand and robot outline with main colors and applied finishing touches, small details, and text in Adobe Photoshop.

A final project for the Design Lab class where students were tasked with creating a futuristic poster of any choice.

The Raven

Used reference images to draw a grayscale raven in Adobe Illustrator, paying particular attention to lights, shadows, contrast, and tiniest details.

“The Raven” is project for the Design Lab class as part of practicing the usage of the software, Adobe Illustrator.

Pressure of the World

Created basic outline in Adobe Photoshop and filled it in with text in Adobe Illustrator. Manipulated text to create an organic, dynamic portrait of a man.

An assignment for the Design Lab class for which students were required to make text-based images from reference.

Serene Nature

A combination of pictures of birds – personally separated from their respective backgrounds – a photograph of a forest, abstract shapes, textures, and various filters in Photoshop. Explores softer color pallet, value, contrast, and balance.

Created for a personal Facebook/LinkedIn page banner.

Llama Power!

Utilized a photograph of a llama, text, and filters in Adobe Photoshop. Separated the llama from the background and altered the colors using tools in the program.

A T-shirt design for a personal request of a fellow student.
